Curricular Aspects

The Department of Vocation (B.Voc) ensures effective curriculum delivery through a well planned and documented process.

1: The Curriculum of B.Voc is Designed and Developed by the faculty members of Ramanujan College .

2: Besides including subjects from specific discipline as per the course , B.voc curriculum also includes an Ability Enhancement Compulsory Course encompassing various subjects like environmental studies , English communication , soft skills , business communication , hindi , life skills , which helps our students to improve their verbal, non-verbal and written language skills.

3: Summer And Winter Internships are a mandatory part of the curriculum.

4: The seamless execution of curriculum delivery is ensured detailed Lesson Plan/SED for each paper and appropriate pedagogy for each topic is identified integrated with ICT-based methods of flipped classroom and blended learning.

5: As a measure to enrich the curriculum delivery , our teachers participate in faculty enrichment programmes/workshops.

6: 6. Our teachers also participate in Setting of question papers for UG/PG programs as well as Assessment /evaluation process
